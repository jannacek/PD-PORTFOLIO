11 Portfolio structure · two reading paths

Assessment Map

Navigation guide for the assessment committee: trajectory reading and assessment reading

This portfolio can be read in two ways: as a trajectory (how the research developed) or as an assessment (how formal criteria are met). Both paths are valid; both lead to the same place.

The PD Arts + Creative programme distinguishes between the four roles (Researcher, Innovator, Change Agent, Professional — see Four Roles) and the five learning goals of the Dutch PD framework (doing artistic/design research; acting professionally; co-creating innovations; enabling change; disseminating for specific contexts). Both frames apply to this portfolio.

Reading Paths

Trajectory path: Home → PD Trajectory → AT-LAB Methodology → Boundary Concepts → EMI Framework → AT-LABs I–IV → Synthesis. This path follows the research logic from first principles to full articulation. Recommended for supervisors and committee members who want to understand the intellectual argument before verifying formal requirements.

Assessment path: Assessment Map (this page) → Four Roles → Supervision → Training & Curriculum → Research ↔ Teaching → Dissemination → Evidence Archive. This path moves directly through formal requirements and provides direct links to evidence.
