Teaching Responsibilities

Teaching responsibilities are formally allocated at 12.5% of total PD time. Teaching at the Frank Mohr Institute constitutes the primary site for AT-LAB II and is not a separate contribution to the research—it is integral to the research trajectory.

  • 2023: 1 EC "Discursive Context: AI in Artistic Practice" (June 2023, Frank Mohr Institute). Initial iteration exploring preliminary research questions.
  • 2024: 2 EC "Artificial Intelligence: In, Within, and Around Your Practice" (April–June 2024, Frank Mohr Institute, expanded to multiple programmes: MADTech, Painting, MAP, IRAP masters). This is AT-LAB II in its inaugural, fully structured form. Includes structured questionnaire, curatorial exhibition, and formal reflection cycle.
  • 2025: Second iteration of 2 EC course at Frank Mohr Institute. Findings from AT-LAB II integrated into course design; ongoing research environment.
